2.Make sure your keypad is working! it uses the keys
0 and .(period) for acceleration and braking respectively 
Title: Tank Armageddon

What to expect from this game:
A sense of frustration at first definitely. We purposefully made
the controls limiting for both the 1st and second player so that they
would have to work together to win. 
Examples of Limitations: 
180 degree rotation of turret for 1st player
Lack of aiming for 2nd player;

If the 1st player didn't aim, then they would be hopeless against enemies from behind.
If the 2nd player wouldn't move, then dodging bullets would be impossible.
It is a game that is NOT meant to be played with just one person for certain,
and once both players beat the game, then they will feel a sense of accomplishment.

FURTHERMORE, as the levels progress. Both players will feel more comfortable moving around
which is why the time limit is decreased because player must learn to be on the OFFENSIVE
in order to win.

Goal: 
Kill a certain amount of enemies before time runs out for each level.
You also have to survive the onslaught of enemies with your trusty pistol and
reloadable shotgun 

Controls:
1st player is on the left:
1.1st player used WASD keys to rotate the turret position to aim 
They can press the Q button to shoot a shotgun which shoots 3 bullets at once

2.IMPORTANT: Turret does NOT rotate 360 degrees. To hit enemy from behind, you must
communicate with your partner to have him point you in the right direction.

2nd player is on the right: (WARNING complicated directions)
1.Player must HOLD the up or down key in order to move in that directions 
2.Left and Right keys DO NOT move the tank. They simply rotate the tank's direction
THEN you use the up or down keys to go forward or backwards in that direction 
3.You MUST HOLD the acceleration key along with the direction you're going in 
in order to speed up. 
We purposefully made the movement  without acceleration to force the player 
to use both hands(one on arrow keys the other on 0 and .) to steer correctly.
4.HOLD the brake key to brake the vehicle

Enemies are:
Tank enemy: Enemy has 3 health. Splits into 3 split_soldiers upon death. 
High priority because killing them can help rack up the score. 

1 point for destroying it.
1 point for every soldier that spawns

Plane Enemy: Enemy has 2 health. 
They will drop bombs if you try to get close to them which can also destroy the buildings.
Use this to your advantage to give your player more moving room!

Flying enemies give 1-2 points depending on how fast you kill them.

Soldier v2: Regular soldier who will run up to the player 
until it reaches a certain distance, then it starts shooting when in range

1 point for destroying it  

Split_soldier: A more modified soldier that spawns from the tank 
enemy who will aggressively pursue the player

1 point for destroying it
